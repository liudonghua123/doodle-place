About doodle-place

doodle-place is an online world inhabited by animate doodles. You can wander around and view doodles created by users around the globe, or contribute your own.

Made by Lingdong Huang. View source or remix on Glitch.

Instructions

  • WASD keys (or use on-screen game pad on touch devices) to move around.
  • N key (or click NEW DOODLE) to add your own doodle.
